# Methodology

## Objective

To simulate droplet impact dynamics using reduced-order physics.

## Assumptions

* Axisymmetric droplet
* Smooth solid surface
* Negligible air resistance
* Constant fluid properties

## Governing Parameters

* Density (ρ)
* Viscosity (μ)
* Surface tension (σ)

## Dimensionless Numbers

* Weber number (We)
* Reynolds number (Re)

## Modeling Approach

* Analytical impact velocity
* Semi-empirical spreading law
* Exponential recoil model

## Limitations

* No full Navier-Stokes solution
* No turbulence modeling
* No splash breakup

## Future Work

* Add crown splash regime
* Include surface roughness
* Couple with CFD solvers
